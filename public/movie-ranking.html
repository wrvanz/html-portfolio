Favorite Movies According to Me!

My top 3 favorite movies of all time.

Dead Poets society

Maverick teacher John Keating returns in 1959 to the prestigious New England boys' boarding school where he was once a star student, using poetry to embolden his pupils to new heights of self-expression.

A Christmas Story

This one is a classic. The film is about a boy named Ralphie Parker who wishes for an official Red Ryder Carbine-action 200-shot Range Model Air Rifle for Christmas. He tries to get this BB gun while getting through childhood problems like two menacing bullies, a protective mother, grumpy father, a whiny younger brother, and a mean Santa Claus.

Eat Pray Love

This is an adaptation of a book written by Elizabeth Gilbert. The story is about a woman's journey of self-discovery through her travels to Italy, India and Indonesia. It's light hearted but reflective.
